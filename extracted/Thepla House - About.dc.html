Our story

Made by Tejal, like she makes it at home

Thepla House by Tejal's Kitchen began in Mumbai in 2018 with one belief — eating out shouldn't mean eating junk. Every dish is still cooked the way Tejal would make it for her own family.

It started with theplas rolled at home and shared with friends who kept asking for more. What they loved wasn't just the taste — it was knowing exactly what went in: 100% whole-wheat atta, sunflower oil, and not a trace of maida or preservatives.

Today that home kitchen has grown into seven kitchens across Mumbai and a menu of 250+ dishes — but the promise hasn't changed one bit.

— Tejal, founder
